About



It is a website made by two Korean students studying at Syracuse University. Many students come and go as time passes, and we wanted to share our list of good restaurant that we discovered over the past few years. Since we have no car, we often ordered or walked to get food, so out of tremendous amount of restaurants available in Syracuse, we picked some of the best and the most accessible restaurants for students without a car in Syracuse.



- Jed Sim & Gene Hong
